Stock Price Prediction

For practice purpose, we will using time series to predict the stock price of Tesla (TSLA), Nvidia (NVDA), and Microsoft (MSFT).

I selected MSFT, NVDA and TSLA for the comparison of multiple stock for the visualization package. Hover over the graph to see the difference.

in this way the graph provide more details and a better understanding for the viewer.

Bitcoin price prediction (single ticker)

For a better comparison between different data visualization package (ggplot, plotly) in R, we use the single ticker bitcoin (BTC) to demonstrate the difference between each packages.

Basic line plot (ggplot)

There’s no hovering for the ggplot, just basic line plot shows the trend of bitcoins.

Basic Line Plot for Bitcoin Price (Plotly)

Plotly line plot provide details by hovering over the graph, is better for the audience to understand the specific price of bitcoin at certain time spot.

Candlestick Chart

The candle stick chart demonstrate the open and close price of bitcoin, or other stock ticker of each day, whchi is easier for us to understand the change of price for each day.
