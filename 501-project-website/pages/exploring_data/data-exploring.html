Twitter Text Data visualization

The source of the data:

Brief introduction: One of the data sources will be the Twitter Tweets, people tend to share thier frustration and unfortunate experience online and there are also twitter account specialized in collecting records of car breakins, especially around San Francisco. The data I'm pulling fram is from the twitter accound @SFCarbreakin, who has been actively collecting people's experience for years. I have used two way to visualized the tweets, one is rating the key word and creating a corresponding cloud and another one is using the heat map to present the correlation between each words.

The rating and word cloud:

I believe word_cloud is an clear and obvious way to demonstrate the key words for this project. After pulling in the tweets, I remove all the puncuations and common stop words to avoid possible interferance of the data, then I use the SentimentIntensity Analyzer to run an rating apprioximation of the tweets data, then use the word cloud to present the results. Which in this case, the top negative words are mostly "broken", "car", "steal","window"

The rating and word cloud:

The heatmap on the other hand was use to demonstrate the correlation between key words like "break", "SF", "car", "smashed". Which don't show a strong correlation but just some minor relationship between the words like "car" and "break", "window" and "smashed", this give the audiance a better understanding between how these cases are often strongly related to the city of San Francisco.
